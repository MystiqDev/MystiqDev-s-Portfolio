About me

I'm an 18-year-old Front-End Web Developer from Oman. I develop clean, modern, and user-friendly interfaces, with great attention to visual detail, responsiveness, and usability across a wide range of screen sizes and devices. I mostly work with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I am always improving my skills by building real projects, trying out new designs, and learning best practices used in the industry. I may still be in my growing and training process, but I take every project seriously and put in genuine effort to produce quality results. As for now, I'll be sticking with front-end work, but my main ambition is to become a full-stack developer in the near future. Motivated, It would be an honor to have the opportunity to work with you and grow in real-world experience.

Services I provide

  • Responsive Design

    I build websites that automatically adjust to different screen sizes, including desktops, tablets, mobile phones, and maybe even your smart fridge. The layout stays clean, readable, and easy to use on any device, so users get a smooth experience no matter how they visit your site.

  • Quick Development

    My development process is fast and efficient, allowing projects to move forward quickly. At the same time, I make sure every detail is polished and the final result meets high quality standards.

  • Interactive Pages

    A website shouldn’t feel static or boring. I carefully add animations, hover effects, and interactive elements that bring your pages to life while keeping everything smooth and user-friendly.

  • Functional Pages

    Not everything is about visuals. I make sure your pages actually do something useful, with some functionalities that fits your goals instead of pointless features.
